This App was developed as a part of my Master Thesis in 2018 and then redesigned at my study abroad. Its purpose is to enable easy cross-platform-learning with flash cards.

Pauker is a text based flash card application. It uses a combination of ultra-short term, short term, and long term memory.
Mobile Pauker++ is an Android version of Pauker and compatible with the version 1.7 and above.

Usage

It is very easy to use. You have the choice to

  • add immediately cards and then save the Lesson, or
  • associate the App with your Dropbox account. Dropbox creates a folder at your account at "Apps" called "Mobile Pauker++". Then just save your lesson on your PC in this folder and synchronise them.
